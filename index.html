Who am i

Hello

My nami is Viktor Kostadinoski. I am 20 years student who comes from a small town in Macedonia called Prilep. I started my education in my hometown where I finished middle and high school. Last year I moved in Skopje where I started studying at the Faculty of computer science and engineering.

I always loved computers and everything technology-related. Even as i child I would sit in front of our old computer and would try to understand how does that magic-box work. But, even tho I loved computers since I was a kid, I didn't start learning something usefully about them, not until later in life. I got into coding at a relatively "old" age - in my first year in high school. At first, I was terrified but after some lessons I got the hang of it. Couple more lessons went by and I knew that that is something I want to do for the rest of my life... So here is my doing it ;)
